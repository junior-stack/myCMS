# 1. Charter of accounts
account type:
- Balance sheet:
	- Income account: revenue of sales, services, etc.
	- Expense account: cost of goods
- Profit & loss:
	- Asset account: assets owned by companies, including tangible assets, accounts receiveable
	- Liability accounts: money owed to others, including accounts payable, bills
- Equity account: Retained earning, money or equity to support company by business owner

Retained earning:

Bill & expense:
- bills: amounts owed to vendor or suppliers
- expense

Reporting methods: sets of rules determining when revenue and expenses are recognized
- cash: recorded when cash changes hands
- accrual: recorded when earned/incurred

journal entry(bank register): use this whenever reconcile bank transactions

reconcil: resolve discrepancies between banking transaction statements and quickbooks transactions

cause of discrepancies:
- missing transactions
- Duplicate transaction
- Incorrect amounts(a quickbook txn may defer from banking statements)

Common ways to resolve reconcilliation under 3 causes:
- missing txns: add the txn
- duplicate txn: void the duplicate txn
- incorrect amounts: edit and adjust the amount
